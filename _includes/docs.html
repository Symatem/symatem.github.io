Documentation

Symatem is about thinking differently about computers: The way we use them, store data, write programs and develop software. If you give it a try, you will find out soon, that even though some things are familiar others are completely alien to you.

I recommend this WBW Post about thinking differently in general.

I tried to break things down to their basic elements and rearrange them in a useful way regardless of how most others are doing them. Thats why Symatem is not just a:

  • Programming language
  • Database
  • Ontology Engine
  • Filesystem
  • Operating-System
It is a unique way of combining all of those into one.
